Day 4

We have visited two industry on the day 4. First one is the Arumugam Spinning Mill and the other one is Venkateshwara Palkova Factory. We played multilanguage antakshari while we are travelling.

ARUMUGAM SPINNING MILL

We visited to the mill unit which has the done the yarn cleaning and bleaching process. This spinning mill is working for 24*7. Most of machines are automatic and manufactured in India and Germany. The installation of the raw materials are done using the skilled workers. Machines are very user friendly, so no workers get injured. There are many interesting machineries have colour sensors and 52 cameras which detect the odd colour yan or dust particles and remove it by air flow. This mill produce cotton threads to further medicinal purpose, cloths manufacturing units and many more.

 

VENKATESHWARA PALKOVA FACTORY

PALKOVA is very famous sweet of the Srivilliputhur, Tamil Nadu. This is the sweet which is made by only milk and sugar, by continuous boiling and stirring. They are using automated machineries for stirring purpose and boiling is done under temperature of 80-90 degree celcius. At the and of the visit they provided sample sweet for us and we also bought for hostels.
