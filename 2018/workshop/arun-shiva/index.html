The working of the Time Teacher is that the random time will be shown in the LCD display and the neopixel starts to rotate the kid who is playing should press the button twice for the hour needle and minute needle.First it will show the hour needle and if the button pressed it will turn red and the minute needle starts to rotate. The kid should press at the correct time else the result will be printed as the sorry try new else it will print good in the LCD display.
