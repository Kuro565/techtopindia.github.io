They give all the cow dung and urine to the medical person to use it and what other thing I observed i.e.

That there are the two species of cows available i.e. 1. Jersey and 2. Indian.

There are many differences in these two species.

The jersey cows give a milk count around 35-40 litres milk in a one day of compare to the Indian cow which gives only 5-10 litres a day.

The jersey cow has a sort life span than that of Indian cow as the injection is given to jersey to increase the quantity of milk.

The jersey cow also has a different shape like of its shoulder, horns and neck than Indian cow.

The jersey cows have less protein and calcium in it’s milk and other one Indian cows having all these quality in just same quantity of milk and it’s milk is also so strong and so good for a born child.

The jersey cow’s milk has a low price rate compare to Indian cow.

There is arrangement for the cow food they are using the process hydrophonic to grow the cow food in any climatic condition. they use water for it and produce a corn type food for cow. They also are growing crop there and some of the papaya’s trees and they are also growing super naferal grass which has a quality that when we cut that grass it will automatically grow again upto 3 years.
