DAY 1

This was the first day of Techtop National innovation workshop which has hosted at Kalasalingam university.I was surprised to know that there were participants from many different states like  Gujarat,Maharashtra,Andhra Pradesh,Tamil Nadu etc.The workshop was mentored by Rajesh Nair from MIT(USA) and Dhruv sasidhar from Ahmedabad.The workshop began with basic introduction on innovation and creativity by Rajesh sir and he later moved on to the problems we face during design thinking process.He constantly urged us to think out of the box by presenting us with different scenarios.The participants were then split into groups with each team having at least 4 members.The team was made by assigning coders,artists and hustlers to each team.I was teamed up with Kishan,Aravindhan and Kaustubh.Later on he taught us the method of tabulating the situations and different scenarios by writing them under different categories like TYPES,ACTIVITY,WHERE,OPERATION AND OUTCOME.He then told us to find solutions to reduce the use of plastic by using this method.

After Lunch we had designing and coding classes.We were taught how to use the SKETCH-UP software and were told to design something which they would 3d print for us.I designed an army styles locket with my name on it.

After this we moved on to arduino where rajesh sir taught us some basics and then told us to sequence the blinking of the LED on the arduino, which we did with ease.At the end of the day we were told to make a project which toddlers could use for playing and the project should be submitted the next day by noon.

                

After dinner members of my group sat till 1 am but we still couldn't fix on a topic for the project.

DAY 2[a]

We began today's session at 9am.We started working on our project .We decided to do a project that could help toddlers to learn different shapes and colour.We used an arduino,20x4 lcd display and a color sensor for this purpose.It was quite difficult to code the arduino so that it would receive the input from the color sensor and display the output on the lcd display.To make things worse i was given the task of coding.After referring google i was able to make the circuit connection  and with a little bit of help from my friend i completed the coding process.While i was working on the code aravind made the physical model,kausthubh made the 3D model and kishan made the video and the presentation .

This was the proposed model which had LED screens on all sides.The screens would show different shapes and colours which the kid can learn with the help of the screen.After 5 hours of non stop work we completed our project .As each member was given different roles we were able to complete the project on time.But our video screwed up as we hadn’t completed the project when the video was made.In the end the project could help toddlers to learn different colours.For this purpose we borrowed a solved rubix cube from the mentors and mapped the red ,green and blue colours intensity to the arduino program.Then we had a demonstration where a group had to present their projects working to every other members including the mentors and Rajesh sir.After the demonstration we presented our project and Rajesh sir praised us for our effort.

After dinner we had an session on Fablab where dhruv sir explained to us about digital fabrication and the credit it provides to our CV.After this we had an ice breaking session where we played a game like Dumsharat where one person had to act an event and his team members had tojoin him in acting.It was quite fun.The days programme ended at 11:30 pm.
