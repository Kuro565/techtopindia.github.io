Day 2

The day 2 began with a rush where each and everyone were busy working with their own toys. We had to do a presentation, a video and then at the show our product.

 Our product was a toy that can teach a toddler to read time. We used a neoPixel, an arduino uno, a LCD display and a switch. The toy will display digital time randomly and the kid has to press the button . A 16 pixel circular neopixel was used and in that the a green light will move from one pixel to another and each pixel indicated the numbers on analog clock. Then based on the number the user has to push the button which will change the colour of the pixel to red and same thing was repeated for minutes. Then the results would be given.

 Then the task will again be started from beginning. After the presentation and completion of the task we had a session on FAB Design and were introduced to the FAB Academy. The day ended after a few games like dubsmash.
